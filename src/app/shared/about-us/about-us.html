About Us

ReadGoodBooks.in portal works under the aegis of North East Digital Consultancy Services, a small consultation and publication enterprise based out of the state of Sikkim.

It is often observed that authors from the Himalayan region and the North East receive very little compensation despite going through the extensive process of book design, printing, launching, hosting, marketing, and distribution.

The commitment of “100% royalty” often translates to only 10–15% of the book cost after deducting various expenses and charges.

North East Digital Consultancy Services and ReadGoodBooks.in evolved from this ethos — with a clear vision to provide an affordable and transparent platform for talented and budding writers.

Our mission is to empower authors by offering a reliable platform for book publication, online marketing, and selling — ensuring they receive fair recognition and value for their work.
